Top Features

New Engine: Power and Acceleration

The new engine maintains the essential character of 2015, but offers a stronger low-mid range for increased acceleration coming out of corners.

New Frame: Lighter handling Chasis

The lighter crankshaft and a number of changes to the chassis deliver a much lighter-handing machine. The noticeably improved corner entry and flickability contribute to quicker lap times.

WSB-DEVELOPED SUSPENSION

The Ninja ZX-10R features a new front fork jointly developed with Showa in the Superbike World championship.
